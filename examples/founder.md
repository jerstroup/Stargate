## Problem
Professional services firms (consultancies, agencies, boutique law) lose 4-7 hours/week per billable employee to meeting notes, follow-ups, CRM hygiene, and time entry. Existing meeting tools (Fireflies, Granola) solve recording but not the downstream work — updating the deal, billing the hour, briefing the partner.

## Customer
Operations partners at 20-200 person professional services firms. We sell top-down to the COO / Head of Ops, land with one practice group, expand firm-wide within 90 days.

## Wedge
A meeting agent that writes the note *and* does the work downstream — CRM updates, time entries, partner briefings — through MCP integrations with the PSA tools these firms already pay for (Clio, Kantata, Rocketlane).
